<p><p>The 2D sensor market is segmented into two main types based on scanning angles: 270° scanning angle sensors and 360° scanning angle sensors. The 270° sensors are designed for applications where a wide field of view is necessary but full circular coverage is not required, making them suitable for specific monitoring tasks.
