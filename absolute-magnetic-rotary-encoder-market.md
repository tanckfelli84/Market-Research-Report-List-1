<p><p>An Absolute Magnetic Rotary Encoder is a device that converts the angular position or motion of a shaft into a digital signal using magnetic technology. Unlike incremental encoders, absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each angle, enabling precise measurements and eliminating the need for a reference point after power loss. These encoders are widely employed in sectors such as industrial automation, robotics, aerospace, and automotive applications due to their robustness, resistance to environmental factors, and high accuracy.</p><p>The Absolute Magnetic Rotary Encoder Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.6% during the forecast period.
