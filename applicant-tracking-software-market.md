<p><p>The Applicant Tracking Software (ATS) market is divided into two primary types: on-premises and cloud-based systems. On-premises ATS is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization, but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based ATS solutions are hosted on remote servers, providing easier accessibility, scalability, and automatic updates. This flexibility allows organizations to streamline recruitment processes without extensive infrastructure, making cloud-based systems increasingly popular among businesses of all sizes.</p></p>
