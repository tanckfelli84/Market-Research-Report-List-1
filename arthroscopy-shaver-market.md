<p><p>The arthroscopy shaver market is primarily segmented into monopole and bipolar systems. Monopole shavers utilize a single electrode for both cutting and coagulation, making them effective for various soft tissue procedures. In contrast, bipolar shavers feature separate electrodes for cutting and coagulating, which helps to minimize thermal damage to surrounding tissues. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of precision, safety, and versatility during arthroscopic surgeries, catering to different surgical needs and preferences among orthopedic specialists.</p></p>
