<p><p>Automated adhesive dispensing systems are essential in various industries for precise and efficient application of adhesives. Cold glue dispensing machines use water-based or solvent-based adhesives, ideal for projects needing slower setting times and easy cleanup. Hot melt dispensing machines, on the other hand, melt solid thermoplastic adhesives, providing quick bonding and strong adhesion on various substrates. Both types enhance production efficiency, reduce waste, and improve product quality, catering to different requirements in manufacturing, packaging, and assembly processes.</p></p>
