<p><ul><li>Fixed Wing</li><li>Multiple Rotor</li><li>Hybrid Wing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automated industrial drones market comprises three main types: fixed wing, multiple rotor, and hybrid wing. Fixed-wing drones offer long-range flights and efficiency for large areas, making them suitable for surveying and agriculture. Multiple rotor drones, or quadcopters, provide versatile maneuverability and are ideal for inspection and monitoring tasks in confined spaces. Hybrid wing drones combine the benefits of both, enabling vertical take-off and landing along with extended flight duration for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
