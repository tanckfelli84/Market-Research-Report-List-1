<p><p>Backup as a Service (BaaS) encompasses three market types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud BaaS utilizes third-party providers' infrastructure, offering scalability and cost-effectiveness for businesses with variable data needs. Private Cloud BaaS provides dedicated resources tailored to specific organizational requirements, enhancing security and control. Hybrid Cloud BaaS combines both models, allowing data to be backed up across public and private infrastructures, offering flexibility and optimized solutions to address diverse data sensitivity and compliance demands.</p></p>
