<p><strong>The Bean Milling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Machine</li><li>Horizontal Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bean milling machine market comprises primarily two types: vertical and horizontal machines. Vertical milling machines utilize a vertically oriented spindle that allows for efficient grinding of beans, ideal for finer particle sizes and improved consistency. In contrast, horizontal milling machines feature a horizontally positioned spindle, which excels in processing larger batches and achieving higher throughput. Each type caters to specific processing needs, with vertical machines often favored for smaller scales and precision, while horizontal machines are preferred for larger-scale operations.</p></p>
