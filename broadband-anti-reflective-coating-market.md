<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Broadband Anti-Reflective Coating market is divided into two main types: single-layer and multilayer coatings. Single-layer coatings consist of one thin film designed to minimize reflection at a specific wavelength, making them effective for narrower spectral ranges. In contrast, multilayer coatings utilize multiple thin films to achieve a wider range of wavelengths, enhancing light transmission across various applications. This versatility makes multilayer coatings popular in sectors like optics, solar energy, and consumer electronics, where broad spectrum performance is essential.</p></p>
