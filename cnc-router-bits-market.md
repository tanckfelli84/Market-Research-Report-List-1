<p><strong>The CNC Router Bits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upcut Spiral Router</li><li>Compression Spiral</li><li>Downcut Spiral Router</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CNC router bits market features various types tailored for specific cutting needs. Upcut spiral routers pull chips upwards, ideal for efficient material removal and cleaner finishes on softer woods. Compression spiral routers combine upcut and downcut designs, minimizing tear-out and producing smooth edges on both sides, perfect for laminates and plywood. Downcut spiral routers push chips downwards, which is beneficial for softer materials, preventing chipping on the top surface and ensuring a clean finish during the cutting process.</p></p>
