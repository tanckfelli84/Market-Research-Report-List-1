<p><p>The Contact Combustion Gas Sensor market features two primary types: Direct Contact Combustion Sensors and Catalytic Contact Combustion Sensors. Direct Contact Combustion Sensors operate by measuring the temperature change due to combustion, providing real-time gas concentration data. In contrast, Catalytic Contact Combustion Sensors utilize catalytic reactions to detect combustible gases, typically providing higher sensitivity and specificity. Both sensor types are essential for applications in safety monitoring, industrial processes, and environmental protection, ensuring efficient and safe gas management.</p></p>
