<p><p>Crystal clock oscillators are essential components in electronic circuits, providing stable frequency references. They are categorized into two main types based on their mounting techniques: SMD (Surface Mount Device) or SMT (Surface Mount Technology) and Through Hole. SMD/SMT oscillators are compact and suitable for automated assembly, offering high-density packaging, making them ideal for modern electronic devices. In contrast, Through Hole oscillators feature leads that pass through the PCB, providing robust connections, often used in applications requiring higher durability and ease of prototyping.</p></p>
