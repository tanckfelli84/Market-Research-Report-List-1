<p><p>The digital printer ink market encompasses various types tailored for specific applications. Reactive dye inks are ideal for vibrant textile printing, bonding chemically with fibers. Acidic inks excel in printing on protein-based materials and synthetic fabrics, producing bright colors. Paint inks, often used in industrial applications, offer durability and adhesion on various surfaces. Dispersion inks are suitable for printing on non-porous substrates, providing excellent color reproduction. Sublimation inks, commonly used for polyester fabrics, allow for high-quality, long-lasting prints through a heat transfer process.</p></p>
