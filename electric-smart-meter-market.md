<p><p>The Electric Smart Meter market is categorized into two main types: single-phase smart meters and three-phase smart meters. </p><p>Single-phase smart meters are designed for residential and small commercial applications, measuring electricity consumption for single-phase power supply. They are generally simpler and more cost-effective.</p><p>Three-phase smart meters cater to larger commercial and industrial users, measuring consumption across three-phase power systems. They offer advanced features for monitoring and managing energy usage, making them essential for efficient power distribution in high-demand settings.</p></p>
