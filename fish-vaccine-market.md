<p><p>The fish vaccine market is segmented into three main types: injectable vaccines, dip vaccines, and oral vaccines. Injectable vaccines are administered directly via injection, providing targeted immune responses. Dip vaccines involve immersing fish in a vaccine solution, offering a practical method for larger populations. Oral vaccines are delivered through feed, allowing easy administration, especially in extensive aquaculture setups. Each type addresses specific health challenges in fish farming, improving fish welfare and enhancing production efficiency in aquaculture.</p></p>
