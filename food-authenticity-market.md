<p><strong>The Food Authenticity Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PCR-Based</li><li>LC-MS/MS</li><li>Isotope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food authenticity market comprises various types of analytical techniques to ensure food products are genuine and unadulterated. PCR-based methods utilize polymerase chain reaction to identify specific DNA biomarkers, effectively detecting species or variety. LC-MS/MS, or liquid chromatography-tandem mass spectrometry, analyzes chemical compounds for detailed profiling and contamination detection. Isotope analysis evaluates the ratio of stable isotopes to authenticate geographical origin or production methods. Together, these methods safeguard consumer trust and enhance food quality verification.</p></p>
