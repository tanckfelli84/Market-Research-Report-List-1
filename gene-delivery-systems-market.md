<p><p>The gene delivery systems market comprises three main types: viral gene delivery systems, non-viral gene delivery systems, and combined hybrid systems. Viral gene delivery utilizes modified viruses to effectively transport genetic material into host cells, offering high efficiency but raising safety concerns. Non-viral systems, such as liposomes or polymers, provide safer options but may have lower delivery efficiency. Combined hybrid systems integrate both viral and non-viral methods, aiming to optimize delivery effectiveness while minimizing risks, thus catering to diverse therapeutic needs.</p></p>
