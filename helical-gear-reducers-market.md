<p><p>The helical gear reducers market is typically categorized into two main types: parallel-axis and perpendicular-axis helical gear reducers. Parallel-axis helical gear reducers feature shafts aligned parallelly, allowing for efficient torque transfer and compact designs, making them suitable for various industrial applications. Conversely, perpendicular-axis helical gear reducers have shafts oriented at right angles, facilitating more flexible layout configurations and often used in applications where space constraints exist. Both types enhance performance by reducing speed and increasing torque in machinery.</p></p>
