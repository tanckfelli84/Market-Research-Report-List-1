<p><strong>The High Speed Photodetector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photon Detector</li><li>Thermal Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed photodetector market is primarily divided into two types: photon detectors and thermal detectors. Photon detectors, such as avalanche photodiodes and photomultiplier tubes, rely on photon interactions to generate electrical signals, offering rapid response times ideal for telecommunications and imaging applications. Thermal detectors, on the other hand, measure temperature changes resulting from absorbed photons, providing sensitivity and broad spectral response but typically at slower speeds. Both types cater to diverse applications in communication, medical, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
