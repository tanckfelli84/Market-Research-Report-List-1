<p><p>The hydraulic wrench market comprises primarily two types: drive hydraulic torque wrenches and hollow hydraulic torque wrenches. Drive hydraulic torque wrenches utilize a ratcheting mechanism for high-torque applications, offering efficiency and precision in fastening tasks. Hollow hydraulic torque wrenches, on the other hand, are designed with a hollow shaft to accommodate long bolts or pipes, allowing for torque application in tight spaces. Both types cater to various industrial sectors, enhancing productivity and ensuring accurate torque specifications.</p></p>
