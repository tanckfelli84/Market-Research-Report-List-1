<p><p>The indoor LED video walls market is categorized based on pixel pitch, which determines resolution and viewing distance. P2mm, P3mm, P4mm, P5mm, P6mm, and P7.62mm refer to the distance between two adjacent pixels in millimeters, with smaller numbers indicating higher resolution. P2mm offers the sharpest images for close views, while P7.62mm is suitable for larger venues where viewers are farther away. The "Others" category includes varied pixel pitches not typically classified under these standard measurements, catering to specific application needs.</p></p>
