<p><p>The industrial robot speed reducer market comprises three main types: RV reducers, harmonic reducers, and planet speed reducers. Harmonic reducers offer precise motion control and low backlash, suitable for applications requiring high accuracy. Planet speed reducers feature multiple gears that distribute load evenly, providing high efficiency and durability.
