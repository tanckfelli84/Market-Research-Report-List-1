<p><p>Industrial X-ray Inspection Systems encompass various technologies, notably Digital Radiography (DR) and Computed Tomography (CT). DR uses digital sensors to capture high-resolution images, offering real-time analysis and faster inspections for quality control in manufacturing. In contrast, CT combines multiple X-ray images to create detailed three-dimensional representations of internal structures, suitable for complex evaluations. Both methods enhance defect detection, material integrity assessments, and process optimization across industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics, ensuring enhanced safety and efficiency.</p></p>
