<p><strong>The Intracranial Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-expandable Stents</li><li>Balloon-expanded Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intracranial stents market features key types: self-expandable stents and balloon-expanded stents. Self-expandable stents, made from flexible materials, are designed to expand and maintain vessel patency upon deployment, adapting to different vessel shapes. In contrast, balloon-expanded stents are deployed using a balloon catheter to achieve precise expansion, providing a rigid structure that supports the artery walls. Both types are crucial in treating conditions like cerebral aneurysms and stenosis, enhancing patient outcomes in neurosurgery.</p></p>
