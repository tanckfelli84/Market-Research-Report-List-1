<p><p>ITO sputtering targets are essential materials used in the deposition of indium tin oxide films for various electronic applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, offering uniform coating over substrates, typically favored for smaller-scale applications. In contrast, rotating targets provide higher deposition rates and improved uniformity for larger surface areas, making them ideal for high-volume manufacturing processes. Both target types cater to distinct operational needs in the electronics industry.</p></p>
