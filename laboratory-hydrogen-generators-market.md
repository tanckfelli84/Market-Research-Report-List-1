<p><ul><li>Membrane Technology</li><li>PSA Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory hydrogen generators market features two primary technologies: Membrane Technology and PSA (Pressure Swing Adsorption) Technology. Membrane Technology utilizes selective membranes to separate hydrogen from other gases, providing a continuous supply of high-purity hydrogen. PSA Technology, on the other hand, involves compressing gas mixtures and cyclically adsorbing impurities on adsorbent materials, allowing for effective hydrogen purification and storage. Both technologies cater to laboratory applications requiring reliable hydrogen sources for various experiments and analysis.</p></p>
