<p><strong>The Laser Safety Goggle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbent Goggles</li><li>Reflective Goggles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser safety goggle market is categorized into two main types: absorbent goggles and reflective goggles. Absorbent goggles are designed to filter and absorb specific wavelengths of laser light, providing protection by preventing harmful exposure directly to the eyes. Reflective goggles, on the other hand, feature surfaces that reflect laser beams, thus redirecting harmful energy away from the eyes. Both types are essential for ensuring safety in environments where lasers are used, such as medical settings, research laboratories, and industrial applications.</p></p>
