<p><p>The medical laser devices market encompasses several types of systems, each with unique applications. Solid-state laser systems utilize solid materials as the gain medium, offering high efficiency and reliability. Gas laser systems, which use gases like CO2, are ideal for soft tissue surgeries due to their precision. Dye laser systems employ organic dyes to produce specific wavelengths, useful for a range of dermatological treatments. Diode laser systems are compact and versatile, making them popular for various applications, including hair removal and dental procedures.</p></p>
