<p><strong>The Medical Vacuum Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Claw Pump Techology</li><li>Dry Rotary Vane Technology</li><li>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical vacuum systems market includes three primary technologies. Dry claw pump technology utilizes two rotating claws to create a vacuum without oil, ensuring cleaner operation and minimal maintenance. Dry rotary vane technology employs rotating vanes within a chamber, eliminating oil use for a more environmentally friendly alternative. Oil-sealed rotary vane technology, on the other hand, relies on oil for lubrication and sealing within the vacuum chamber, offering a higher level of performance but requiring more maintenance due to oil management. Each technology serves specific medical applications and preferences.</p></p>
