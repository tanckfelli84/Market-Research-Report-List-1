<p><p>The Multi-Deck Refrigerated Display Case market comprises two primary types: Plug-In and Semi Plug-In models. Plug-In Multi-Deck cases operate independently, relying on an external power source for refrigeration. They are portable and easy to install, ideal for small to medium retail settings. In contrast, Semi Plug-In models combine both self-contained and central refrigeration units, providing greater energy efficiency and performance. These cases are often used in larger commercial environments, allowing for custom configurations and better integration into existing systems.</p></p>
