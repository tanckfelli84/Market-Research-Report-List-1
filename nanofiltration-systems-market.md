<p><p>Nanofiltration systems are essential in water treatment and various industrial applications, featuring primarily two types: spiral membranes and tubular membranes. Spiral membranes are compact and efficient, utilizing a coiled structure to maximize surface area, making them ideal for pressure-driven processes. Tubular membranes, on the other hand, consist of straight tubes, which provide higher resistance to fouling and are suitable for viscous fluids. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to diverse needs within the nanofiltration market.</p></p>
