<p><p>The Narrow Web Print Quality Inspection System market comprises two main types: In-line Print Quality Inspection Systems and Off-line Print Quality Inspection Systems. In-line systems operate during the printing process, ensuring real-time monitoring and immediate feedback for defect detection, enhancing efficiency and reducing wastage. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining high print quality standards across various applications.</p></p>
