<p><p>The optical circulator market comprises primarily two types: Single-Mode Type and Polarization-Maintaining Type. The Single-Mode Type is designed for use with single-mode fibers, facilitating efficient light transmission with minimal loss, making it ideal for long-distance communication. In contrast, the Polarization-Maintaining Type preserves the polarization state of light, which is critical in applications requiring precise signal integrity, such as in sensor systems and advanced telecommunications. Together, these types address diverse needs in optical networking and signal processing.</p></p>
