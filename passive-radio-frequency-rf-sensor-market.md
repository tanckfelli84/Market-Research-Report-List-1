Low Frequency RF sensors, typically operating below 300 kHz, are used for long-range communication and basic monitoring. Mid Frequency RF sensors, ranging from 300 kHz to 3 MHz, are commonly employed in industrial and automotive sectors for tracking and data transmission. High Frequency RF sensors, above 3 MHz, are utilized in advanced communication systems, medical diagnostics, and high-speed data transfer.</p></p>
