<p><ul><li>Isotonic Physiological Sea Water Nasal Spray</li><li>Hypertonic Physiological Sea Water Nasal Spray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Physiological Sea Water Nasal Spray market includes two primary types: isotonic and hypertonic sprays. Isotonic physiological sea water nasal sprays contain salt concentrations similar to that of bodily fluids, which help to moisturize and soothe nasal passages without irritation. In contrast, hypertonic sprays have a higher salt concentration, promoting osmosis to reduce nasal congestion and draw excess fluid from the mucous membranes. Both types are widely used for allergy relief, sinus issues, and overall nasal hygiene.</p></p>
