<p><p>The plastic trunking market is categorized into two main types: mini trunking and maxi trunking. Mini trunking is designed for small-scale applications, ideal for organizing low-voltage cables in residential or small commercial settings, offering a discreet and space-saving solution. In contrast, maxi trunking accommodates larger volumes of cables and is suited for industrial or extensive commercial environments, providing robust support for power and data cables. Both types enhance cable management and protect wiring from damage, ensuring safety and efficiency.</p></p>
