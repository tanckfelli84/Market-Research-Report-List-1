<p><p>The QR Code Generator market encompasses various types of barcode technology, including Stacked 2D Barcode Generators and Matrix 2D Barcode Generators. Stacked 2D Barcode Generators create codes that store information vertically, allowing for compact designs ideal for limited spaces, such as labels and packaging. In contrast, Matrix 2D Barcode Generators utilize a grid layout to represent data, offering higher data density and error correction capabilities, making them suitable for applications in logistics, retail, and marketing. Both types enhance data encoding efficiency and accessibility.</p></p>
