<p><p>Refrigerated compressed air dryers are essential for removing moisture from compressed air systems. The market is primarily divided into two types: cycling and non-cycling refrigerated air dryers. Cycling refrigerated air dryers utilize a controller to adjust the cooling capacity based on demand, improving energy efficiency and reducing operational costs. In contrast, non-cycling refrigerated air dryers maintain a constant cooling level regardless of airflow, which can lead to higher energy consumption but offers simpler operation and lower initial costs.</p></p>
