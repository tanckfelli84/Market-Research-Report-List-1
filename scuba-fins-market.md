<p><p>The scuba fins market is broadly categorized into open-heeled fins and closed-heeled (full foot) fins. Closed-heeled fins, or full foot fins, are designed for snug fit directly on the foot, offering enhanced propulsion and comfort for warm-water diving. Each type serves distinct preferences and diving conditions.</p></p>
