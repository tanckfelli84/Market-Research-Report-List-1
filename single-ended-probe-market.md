<p><strong>The Single-Ended Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Single-Ended Probes</li><li>Active Single-Ended Probes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-ended probe market is divided into two main types: passive and active single-ended probes. Passive single-ended probes are typically simpler and rely on resistive elements to measure signals without amplification, making them cost-effective for low-frequency applications. In contrast, active single-ended probes incorporate internal amplification, providing improved signal integrity and bandwidth, suitable for higher-frequency measurements. Both types serve essential roles in electronic testing, targeting varying requirements in performance, accuracy, and application specificity across various industries.</p></p>
