<p><p>The smart water leak detectors market comprises two main types: passive and active leak detectors. </p><p>Passive leak detectors are devices that monitor specific areas for signs of water presence, relying on alerts from sensors when leaks occur. They typically require manual intervention. </p><p>Active leak detectors, on the other hand, not only detect leaks but also take action, such as shutting off the water supply. These proactive solutions enhance safety and minimize potential damage, making them increasingly popular in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
