<p><ul><li>On-Grid String Inverter</li><li>Off-Grid String Inverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The string inverter market is divided into two main types: on-grid and off-grid string inverters. On-grid string inverters connect solar systems to the utility grid, allowing excess energy to be fed back for credit or compensation. They are ideal for residential and commercial applications where grid connectivity is available. Off-grid string inverters, on the other hand, operate independently of the grid, storing energy in batteries for use during low solar production periods, making them suitable for remote locations without grid access.</p></p>
