<p><ul><li>Push/Pull Switching Transformer</li><li>Half Bridge Switching Transformer</li><li>Full Bridge Switching Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The switching transformer market comprises various types based on their operational configurations. Push/Pull switching transformers utilize two active devices to drive the transformer’s primary, offering compact design and efficient energy transfer. Half bridge transformers feature two switches that alternate current, providing balanced operation and reduced stress on components. Full bridge transformers employ four switches for improved efficiency and higher power levels, making them ideal for applications requiring robust performance. Each type caters to specific power needs in diverse electronic systems.</p></p>
