<p><strong>The Thermoforming Techniques Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Forming</li><li>Vacuum Forming</li><li>Matched Mold Forming</li><li>Twin Sheet Forming</li><li>Mechanical Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoforming techniques encompass several market types for shaping plastic materials. Pressure forming uses compressed air to form parts with improved detail and durability. Vacuum forming involves heating plastic sheets and using a vacuum to mold them over a shape, ideal for simpler designs. Matched mold forming utilizes two heated molds, enabling complex shapes with high precision. Twin sheet forming involves two heated sheets that are simultaneously formed and joined, optimizing thickness and structural integrity. Mechanical forming employs additional mechanical forces to enhance detail and control.</p></p>
