<p><strong>The Wastewater Evaporators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Floating Evaporators</li><li>Trajectory Evaporators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wastewater evaporators market includes two primary types: floating evaporators and trajectory evaporators. Floating evaporators operate on the surface of wastewater, utilizing natural sunlight and heat to promote evaporation, making them energy-efficient. Trajectory evaporators, on the other hand, spray a thin film of wastewater into the air, enhancing evaporation through increased surface area exposure. Both types effectively reduce liquid waste volume, help manage environmental compliance, and provide sustainable solutions for wastewater treatment in various industrial applications.</p></p>
